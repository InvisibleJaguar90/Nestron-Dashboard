Review Notes
Format
Template 1: Single post with immediate self-reply for the link. The primary post has no link — external links in primary X posts receive an algorithmic reach penalty. The reply carries the link with minimal copy so it's available to engaged readers without penalizing the original post's distribution.
Angle
Contrarian take / industry reframe (see Repurposing Angle Framework in social/platform_guidelines.md). The opening reframe — "factory-built" as a quality specification, not a descriptor — challenges the default way this audience categorizes the product. X rewards posts that are specific enough to generate either agreement or productive pushback. This claim is defensible and non-obvious without being inflammatory.
Characters
~270 characters for the primary post. The platform discipline target is to write as if the limit is 200 characters — this post runs slightly over that target but each sentence is earning its place. If further compression is desired: the third paragraph ("Site-built construction can produce excellent outcomes. It cannot produce the same outcome twice under the same conditions.") could stand alone as the complete post with the first paragraph removed. Both versions are viable.
Hashtag
One hashtag: #ADU. Active, specific, relevant. Placed on its own line at the end. Per guidelines, 1–2 hashtags maximum on X — more reads as low-quality content. No second hashtag needed here; the post's industry positioning doesn't require additional discovery tags.
Timing
Tuesday–Thursday mornings. Post the primary post, then immediately reply with the link before other activity begins on the thread. Early engagement in the first 30 minutes sets the algorithmic trajectory — be available to respond to any replies during that window.
Multipliers
Action items for later: (1) X Premium ($8/mo) provides a 2–4x algorithmic reach multiplier for brand accounts — worth activating before publishing. (2) If the founder's X account can amplify or quote-post this with a personal take, it would outperform anything the brand account produces independently. Neither is a blocker for publishing, but both are worth addressing.
Red Lines
✓ Pass — No ROI claims, no permit guarantees, no timeline commitments, no competitor references. The claim about repeatability is accurate and specific.
